# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Product Positioning
**AI Spend Audit** is the "Lighthouse for AI Costs." It provides a free, instant, and actionable audit of a company's AI tool stack to identify overlapping subscriptions and wasted spend.

## Target Audience
1. **Early-stage Startups**: Spending $500 - $2k/mo on AI but without a dedicated finance/ops lead.
2. **Freelance Developers**: Using multiple tools (Cursor, Copilot, ChatGPT) and looking for the most efficient combo.
3. **Engineering Managers**: Looking to consolidate team subscriptions into business/enterprise plans.

## Distribution Channels

### 1. Organic Search (SEO)
- Target keywords: "Cursor vs Copilot cost", "Is ChatGPT Plus worth it for teams?", "SaaS spend audit".
- Content: Comparative blog posts and "Cost Saving Guides" for specific tools.

### 2. Community Outreach
- **Product Hunt**: Launching as a free tool to build authority and capture leads.
- **Hacker News/Reddit**: Sharing "The Hidden Costs of the AI Stack" data-driven posts.

### 3. Social Media
- **LinkedIn**: Targeting CTOs and Engineering Leads with "How much is your team overspending on AI?" polls.
- **Twitter/X**: Partnering with "AI Influencers" to audit their own stacks.

## Lead Magnet & Conversion
- **Free Audit**: No-signup required to see results.
- **Gated PDF**: High-value, printable report requires email capture.
- **Credex Integration**: High-spend users are qualified for "AI Credit Consultations" (Up to 90% savings).

## Success Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of users who finish the form.
- **Lead Capture Rate**: Percentage of auditors who provide an email.
- **Share Rate**: Percentage of reports shared via unique URL.
