# Key Metrics & KPIs

This document outlines the metrics we track to measure the success and health of the AI Spend Audit platform.

## 1. Funnel Metrics
- **Landing Page Views**: Total unique visitors.
- **Form Completion Rate**: % of visitors who complete the audit form.
- **Lead Capture Rate**: % of users who provide an email to download/share the report.

## 2. Value Metrics
- **Total Savings Identified**: Aggregate sum of potential monthly and annual savings identified across all audits.
- **Average Savings per User**: Total savings / Number of audits.
- **High-Value Leads**: Number of leads identified with >$1,000/mo in potential savings.

## 3. Engagement Metrics
- **Report Shares**: Total number of unique share URLs generated.
- **PDF Downloads**: Total number of reports exported to PDF.
- **Return Visitors**: % of users who revisit a saved report via its shareable URL.

## 4. Performance Metrics (SLA)
- **Audit Generation Time**: Time from form submission to report display (Target: <2s).
- **PDF Generation Speed**: Time to render and download a multi-page PDF (Target: <5s).
- **Lighthouse Performance Score**: Target: >90 across all categories.

## 5. System Health
- **API Error Rate**: % of failed backend requests.
- **Email Delivery Rate**: % of successfully sent transactional emails via Brevo.
- **OpenAI API Latency**: Average response time for AI summary generation.
